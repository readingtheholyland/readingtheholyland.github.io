Not Everyone liked Pilgrimages

March 24, 2023

As the RTHL team goes through catalogues in search of Latin works about the Holy Land written during the crusades, one comes often across interesting things. During the crusades, one of the most popular literary genres on the Holy Land were pilgrimage guides. They were often written by eyewitnesses who often reused material from other sources and accounts.
