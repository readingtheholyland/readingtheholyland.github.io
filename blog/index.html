Not Everyone likes Pilgrimages

April 30, 2021

As the RTHL team goes through catalogues in search of Latin works about the Holy Land written during the crusades, one comes often across interesting things. One of the most popular literary genres of this corpus during the crusader period were pilgrimage guides. They were often written by eye-witnesses who, however, often reused material from other sources and accounts of other pilgrims.
